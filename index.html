Event Overview

The **"Build with AI Owerri"** event, organized by Google Developer Groups (GDG) Owerri, is a hands-on workshop by Google. It focuses on the practical application of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and aims to demonstrate how developers, startups, and businesses can leverage AI to automate workflows, enhance user experiences, and develop smarter solutions.
